Manufacturing Process

Advanced Manufacturing Techniques

Tyre Pyrolysis is the thermal decomposition of End of Life Tyres at elevated temperature in the inert atmosphere in closed loop system. Our Manufacturing Process runs in controlled manner where the dish is positioned every minute. No human formula is make sure that the process is complete and all the safety gears are utilized efficiently to keep the environment safe and secure.

During the Pyrolysis process the ELT's are converted into Pyrolysis Fuel Oil, Carbon Char and Tyre Wires, thereby leaving no wastage whatsoever.

We have one of the leading production capacities world wide, to give you a outlook of the same, we consume about 20000MT of Tyres and Industrial Rubber put together and we produce approximately 16000MT of Pyrolysis Fuel oil, 4000MT of Pyrolysis Carbon and 4000MT of Steel Wires.
